About CEO

CEO’s main focus is to create and support student run entrepreneurial eco-systems on university/college campuses. CEO HQ staffers work one-on-one with students, faculty and campus leaders to support the development of the community and promote entrepreneurship campus-wide.
Why CEO: Students
As a valued member of the Collegiate Entrepreneurs’ Organization network, you’ll be joining a network of thousands of likeminded individuals focused on building a brighter future through new innovation.
